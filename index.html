About Us

Stone Cold Brewery was founded by Natalie Salemme, who started brewing beer in her garage and tweaking the recipe in the hopes of creating the perfect beer. With the help of her friends and family, she created the recipe for "Stonest Coldest", Stone Cold Brewery's famous IPA. Stone Cold Brewery happily welcomes both locals and tourists to try a flight of our hand crafted beer. Currently, we have 4 beers on tap that are brewed in our restaurant and we are constantly trying new recipes to be released year-round. Come by and try a flight!
